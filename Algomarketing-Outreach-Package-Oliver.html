Algomarketing has built one of the most credible embedded marketing talent propositions in the market. The growth model that got it here will not get it to £150M.

The ceiling

The Growth Manager will join and find no enterprise account database, no Account Based Marketing programme, no content flywheel, and no outbound channel.
